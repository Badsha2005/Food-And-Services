Rainbow Buddha Bowl

15 mins
2 servings
4.8 (124)
Vegan Gluten-Free High-Protein Low-Carb

This vibrant Rainbow Buddha Bowl is packed with nutrient-dense ingredients that will nourish your body and delight your taste buds. The combination of fresh vegetables, plant-based protein, and a creamy tahini dressing makes this a perfect meal for any time of day.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories 320 kcal
Protein 18g
Carbs 32g
Fiber 9g
Fat 14g
Sugar 6g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 medium sweet potato, roasted
  • 1 cup chickpeas, roasted
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup purple cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup kale, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Water to thin dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut sweet potato into cubes, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper, and roast for 25 minutes until tender.
  2. Drain and rinse chickpeas, pat dry, then toss with olive oil and your favorite spices. Roast for 20 minutes until crispy.
  3. Prepare quinoa according to package instructions. Let cool slightly.
  4. Massage kale with a bit of olive oil and salt to soften the leaves.
  5. Make the dressing by whisking together tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, garlic powder, and enough water to reach desired consistency.
  6. Assemble bowls by dividing quinoa between two bowls. Arrange sweet potatoes, chickpeas, avocado, cabbage, kale, and tomatoes in sections.
  7. Drizzle with tahini dressing and sprinkle with pumpkin seeds. Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • For extra protein, add grilled tofu or tempeh to your bowl.
  • Prep ingredients ahead of time for quick assembly during busy weekdays.
  • Try different grain bases like brown rice, farro, or cauliflower rice for variety.
  • Add a sprinkle of nutritional yeast for a cheesy, umami flavor boost.
